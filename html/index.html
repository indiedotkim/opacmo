The Open Access Mortar
Powered by 
Find publications linked to genes, proteins, species, diseases and other ontological terms. Start off with entering some biological or biomedical term that you like to search for in the text field below, such as BRCA2, leukemia, lactase activity, Mus musculus. The search starts seamlessly when you enter two or more letters. For example, try entering 'brc'.
Below you see terms that start with the text you entered. Click on one or more terms that you are interested in. If you entered 'brc' as suggested beforehand, you see that it matches the 'BRCA' genes as well as species 'Brachypodium' and OBO term 'breast cancer'. Try clicking on 'BRCA2'.
Now you are presented with a list of publications and their linked genes, proteins, species, diseases, etc. that fit the selected terms above. Enter another term in the text field to narrow down your search further. The terms that you select on top of the current selection will be added to this query conjunctively. You can remove terms from the search by clicking the close button on top of the category in the previous step.

About

opacmo is the Open Access Mortar — a mash-up of biological and biomedical terms linked to publications of the open-access subset of PubMed Central.

opacmo is currently in its development phase. You can read more about it in this blog post.
